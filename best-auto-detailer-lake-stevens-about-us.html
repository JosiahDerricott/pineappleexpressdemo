WHO WE ARE

The Guys
Behind the Detail

We are owned and operated by Jaren Hew and David Turner, who have a passion for bringing joy to others.

Jaren Hew started out small, detailing vehicles out of his own backyard and when the opportunity arose for this side job to become a full time career, Jaren didn't hesitate at the idea and he invited his best friend of 14 years, David Turner, to go into business with him. Pineapple Express Auto Salon was born. We are a small business with a love for our community and the surrounding areas. Our passion, dedication and integrity shine through our work.
