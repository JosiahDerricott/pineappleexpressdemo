Window Tinting

We offer Basic and Ceramic tint. Basic tint will filter a percentage of light and ceramic will filter both a percentage of light and 75% of heat. We can go as low as 20% tint for all cars. Legal limit is 25% for the front windows and no limit for back windows for all trucks, vans and SUV vehicles.

Paint Protection

PPF, commonly known as a clear bra, is a very thin yet durable plastic covering that is applied to the body of the vehicle to protect from minor damage such as chips from gravel on the road and minor scratching. PPF has come a long way and is now so advanced that you won’t even be able to tell it’s there just by looking at it! We can apply the protection film to either one area such as the front end nose or the entire vehicle! PPF can last up to 5-7 years, depending on driving/ weather conditions, and level of care for the film.

Ceramic Coating

A hydrophobic coating that makes cleaning your car a breeze! This coating allows water droplets to bead off the exterior and makes contaminants such as pollen and mildew easier to remove. This is a great coating to use on windshields, wheels and even leather seats! Ceramic coatings can be applied to nearly any surface! Lifespan of the product can be anywhere from 2-5 years depending on quality of care and with frequent upkeep of maintenance.

Detailing

Our detailing packages are designed for every driver out there. From your daily commuter to your work truck or even the family vehicle with kids, pets and heavy use. Either way, we’ve got you covered. We even have a monthly maintenance package, perfect for those Uber or Lyft drivers that need a clean car at all times!

We currently have 3 detail levels plus our maintenance plan. To determine which is best for you, is to contact us so we may talk with you and help you find what you need!
